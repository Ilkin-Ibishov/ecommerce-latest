## Unique Features kontakt.az Has

1. **Aylıq Ödəniş (Monthly Payment)** — installment plans up to 24 months at 0%
2. **Product Comparison Tool** — compare up to 3-4 products side by side
3. **Online Trade-in** — submit old device, get credit
4. **2-hour Delivery** — same-day express in Baku
5. **Price Guarantee** — "find cheaper, get 120% of difference back"
6. **Multiple Warranty Tiers** — manufacturer / gold / plus warranties
7. **Countdown Deals Widget** — live timer for weekly offers
8. **Brand Store Pages** — dedicated pages per brand (Apple Authorized Reseller)
9. **Corporate Sales** — B2B purchasing flow
10. **Loyalty Program ("Qarabağ" proqramı)** — points / cashback
11. **Blog / Content** — SEO-rich product guides
12. **Store Locator** — find physical store on map
13. **WhatsApp / Telegram support** — direct messaging
14. **Social Proof at Scale** — 58k+ views on components, Instagram active
